# Marketing Illustration Prompts

Use these prompts with Midjourney v6 or DALL-E 3 to generate consistent marketing assets for Accessibility Fold.

## 🎨 Art Direction
**Style:** Modern SaaS, Isometric, 3D clay-render style but clean.
**Colors:** Indigo (#4f46e5), White, Slate, Soft gradients.
**Mood:** Professional, Inclusive, Technological, Smooth.

---

## 1. Hero Image (Home)
> **Prompt:** 
> A 3D isometric illustration of a digital webpage folding out like origami, revealing an accessible, structured version underneath. Soft indigo and white color palette. Clean lines, soft lighting, tech-forward. Elements of UI floating around: a magnifying glass, a speaker icon (for screen readers), and a checkmark shield. High quality, 8k render, octane render. --ar 16:9

## 2. Feature: AI Remediation
> **Prompt:** 
> A sleek 3D illustration of a robot arm or a glowing digital brain placing "ALT" tags onto images. The images are floating polaroids. The environment is a clean, white abstract workshop. Indigo glowing accents. Soft shadows. Minimalist. --ar 4:3

## 3. Feature: Widget Customization
> **Prompt:** 
> A floating UI control panel in 3D. Sliders for "Text Size", "Contrast", and "Spacing". A cursor hand is adjusting a slider. The background is a soft gradient blur. Glassmorphism effect on the panel. High fidelity. --ar 4:3

## 4. Compliance & Legal
> **Prompt:** 
> A 3D shield made of glass and glowing indigo light, protecting a stylized website mockup. A calm, secure atmosphere. A document with a wax seal (representing a certificate) is nearby. --ar 4:3

## 5. Dashboard Representation
> **Prompt:** 
> An isometric view of a data dashboard floating in void. Charts showing "Accessibility Score" going up (green/indigo). Clean cards with data points. Abstract connections to little globe icons (representing domains). --ar 16:9

---

## 💡 Usage Tips
*   **Consistency:** Always use `--s 250` (in MJ) for consistent style strength.
*   **Color Correction:** Post-process images to match the exact brand Hex `#4f46e5` if needed.
*   **Backgrounds:** Generate with white or simple backgrounds for easy integration.
